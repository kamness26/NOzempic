Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

NOzempic is a private, personal fitness accountability app used by a closed group of two participants. This policy explains how we handle health and activity data collected from WHOOP and Oura Ring devices.

Data We Collect

We collect health and fitness data from connected wearable devices, including recovery scores, activity strain, sleep performance, and heart rate variability. Body composition data may also be collected via manual uploads.

How We Use Your Data

Data is used exclusively to generate a weekly private health summary email sent only to the participant whose data was collected. Group-level data shared between participants uses relative deltas only — no absolute personal health numbers are shared with other participants.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose your health data to any third parties. Data is processed by the Anthropic API solely to generate personalized coaching text and is not stored or used for training purposes.

Data Storage

Activity data is fetched in real time each week and is not permanently stored. Body composition data uploaded for processing is handled locally and not retained after weekly processing is complete.

Access & Deletion

Participants may revoke WHOOP data access at any time by disconnecting the app in their WHOOP account settings. To request deletion of any stored data, contact the app administrator directly.
